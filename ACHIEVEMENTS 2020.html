Achievements of 2020

1. COVID-19 Pandemic Response

Rapid implementation of health measures to combat the COVID-19 pandemic, including building new hospitals, establishing quarantine facilities, and ramping up PPE production.

2. Digital Government Services

The launch of e-government services, allowing Egyptians to access services like paying bills, applying for permits, and more online.

3. Benban Solar Park Expansion

Further development and expansion of Benban Solar Park, solidifying Egypt's role as a leader in solar energy.

4. Hepatitis C Treatment Program

Continuing efforts to eradicate Hepatitis C, with millions of Egyptians tested and treated.

5. Public Health Screening

Nationwide health screening campaigns for diabetes, hypertension, and other non-communicable diseases.

6. Road Network Improvements

Completion of thousands of kilometers of new and upgraded roads to improve transportation.

7. Cultural Diplomacy

Opening of the Grand Egyptian Museum and the launch of global cultural exchange programs to share Egypt's heritage worldwide.
