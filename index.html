Why do you prefer Egypt

Egypt is a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. It is home to some of the world's most iconic landmarks, such as the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Nile River the longest river in the world. The country has a deep cultural heritage that dates back thousands of years, blending ancient traditions with modern life. Egyptian cuisine, music, and art are full of flavor and creativity, reflecting the diversity of its people. Warm hospitality and strong family values are deeply rooted in society, making Egypt a welcoming and vibrant place admired by many.
